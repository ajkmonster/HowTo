Reminiscing and Retracting  

1.Reminiscing and Retracting

 
  1. You are allowed to cry

    You may not understand everything that you are feeling and still processing, but it's okay to cry and let those emotions out.

  2. You are allowed to watch sad movies or rom-coms

    You can let yourself go back into the fantasy that you are still together and you are allowed to believe that love still exists.

  3. You are allowed to listen to old voice mails

    Sometimes having the memento and the sound of something familiar helps soothe us and that is okay.

  4. You are allowed to relive your memories

    You built something with this person and reliving it is not a bad thing.
