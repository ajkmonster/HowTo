Releasing  

2.Releasing

 
  1. Listen to yourself

    You will only be at this step till you are ready to listen and understand yourself.

  2. Reconnect with some of your close friends

    Remember to keep those close to you in the loop and explain yourself to them.

  3. You'll still miss them

    Let's face it missing them is still going to happen because they were a part of your life. However, at least now you can somewhat control the urge to text or call them.

  4. Keep moving forward

    Everyday is a new day and you have to keep moving, even when life is bringing you down.
