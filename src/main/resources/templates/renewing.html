Renewing  

4.Renewing

 
  1. You've made it

    Clap your hands and pat yourself. You have gotten through a very rough patch.

  2. Focus on you

    You should always put yourself as number 1 and remember that self-care is very important.

  3. Relationship

    It is not always a compromise, but rather being yourself to the fullest and sharing that with another person. Rather than one half and another half, but two wholes that compliment one another.

  4. Go out there

    Be the best version of yourself and conquer the world.
