Rebuilding  

3.Rebuilding

 
  1. Socialize with others

    You start to build yourself again and start going out more.

  2. Improve your habits or life

    Use the new found strength for bettering your life or achieving goals you had set before but never had gotten to them.

  3. Try learning something new

    You have more time with yourself so trying to learn a language or a new skill.

  4. Relapse

    At this point there are times when you think back from however long ago the relationship was and think of the memories, love, and passion in your old relationship. Even though it's hard you have to pull through because you have to remember all the reasons why it didn't work out and all the reasons you have made it to this day.
