How to recover from a heartbreak  

The steps onto recovering from heartbreak

a guide from Wongfu Productions

 

We all have a story...

The people in our lives don't always match what we imagine in our heads. In life that is okay since we take the initiative to compromise and learn from those differences. However there are times that maybe you or the other person may not feel that the relationship is no longer worth the effort. In such cases, heartbreak may occur and there is no cookie cutter way to navigate these emotions, but Wongfu Productions has created a wonderful video depicting one story of such instances. We have the protagonist Carly who has just stumbled into the heartbreak zone after leaving a long time relationship with her now ex-boyfriend. We see the struggle, the emotions, and the turmoil that feeds into her heartbreak. Any heartbreak is not easy, but this one cuts to the core.

Wongfu Productions may not resolve nor depict all the emotions in your journey to conquer heartbreak, but definitely showcases the importance of self reflection, self love, and self growth. This how to recover from heartbreak will give you guidance on steps to moving forward.

 
Steps Description
1.Reminiscing and Retracting This part is the time to heal, to cope and to relive those memories
2.Releasing This part is to slow let go of those feelings and begin to equalize your life
3.Rebuilding This part is to finally take those steps forward to reshaping your life without that person
4.Renewing This part is about honing in on your new self and using that to step into the challenges you have in life
 

Disclaimer: Everyone moves at their own pace and goes through their own process through this journey so the time spent in each step may be different for everybody.
